Meet Chef Maria Rodriguez

Chef Maria brings 15 years of culinary expertise to Savory Bistro, combining classical French techniques with modern American flavors.

Her philosophy is simple: start with the best ingredients from local farms and let their natural flavors shine. Every dish is crafted with care, celebrating the seasons and supporting our community.

Maria's commitment to sustainability and farm partnerships has earned Savory Bistro recognition as one of the city's top sustainable restaurants.

"Cooking is about honoring the ingredients and the farmers who grew them. When you start with quality, the rest is easy."

— Chef Maria Rodriguez
