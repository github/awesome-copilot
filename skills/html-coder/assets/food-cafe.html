Your Community Coffee House

Brew & Bean started in 2018 with a simple mission: create exceptional coffee and a welcoming space where neighbors become friends.

We roast our own beans in small batches, source from ethical sustainable farms, and bake fresh pastries every morning. But what we're most proud of is the community we've built.

Whether you're here to work, meet friends, or simply enjoy a quiet moment with a great cup of coffee, we're glad you chose Brew & Bean.

Our Values

  • Quality: Premium beans, expert roasting, skilled baristas
  • Community: Supporting local artists, hosting events, giving back
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging, composting, green practices
  • Inclusivity: Everyone is welcome at our table
