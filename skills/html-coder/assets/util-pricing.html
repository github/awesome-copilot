Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month or annual (for a discount). You can cancel anytime with no penalties.

What happens when I reach my limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. You can upgrade your plan or remove inactive users/projects.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes! We offer 50% off for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales for details.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, regular security audits, and are SOC 2 Type II certified. Your data is backed up daily.
