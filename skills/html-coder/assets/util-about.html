Our Story

Founded in 2018, our company started with a simple observation: teams were struggling with too many disconnected tools, complicated workflows, and endless meetings.

Three co-founders with backgrounds in software engineering, product design, and organizational psychology came together to build a better solution. We believed there had to be a simpler way for teams to collaborate and get work done.

What started as a weekend project in a San Francisco garage has grown into a platform serving 100,000+ teams worldwide. Along the way, we've stayed true to our core belief: technology should empower people, not complicate their lives.

Today, we're a team of 150+ people across three continents, united by a shared mission to make collaboration effortless for everyone.

Our Mission

To empower every team to achieve more together by providing intuitive, powerful tools that simplify collaboration.
