About Me

I'm a UI/UX designer with 8 years of experience creating digital products that people love to use. Based in San Francisco, I work with startups and established companies to design intuitive, accessible, and beautiful experiences.

My design philosophy is rooted in empathy—understanding users' needs, frustrations, and goals. I believe great design solves real problems while creating moments of delight.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me hiking Bay Area trails, experimenting with film photography, or mentoring junior designers through ADPList.

My Design Process

How I approach every project

01

Discover & Research

Understanding the problem space through user interviews, competitive analysis, and stakeholder workshops. I dig deep to uncover user needs and business constraints.

  • User interviews
  • Competitive analysis
  • Heuristic evaluation
  • Analytics review
02

Define & Strategize

Synthesizing research into actionable insights, creating user personas, journey maps, and defining the product strategy and key metrics for success.

  • User personas
  • Journey mapping
  • Information architecture
  • Success metrics definition
03

Design & Prototype

Exploring multiple solutions through sketching, wireframing, and high-fidelity mockups. Creating interactive prototypes to test ideas and gather feedback early.

  • Sketching & ideation
  • Wireframing
  • Visual design
  • Interactive prototyping
04

Test & Iterate

Conducting usability testing with real users, analyzing results, and refining the design based on feedback. Design is never done on the first try.

  • Usability testing
  • Accessibility audits
  • Design iteration
  • Stakeholder reviews
05

Deliver & Support

Collaborating closely with developers during implementation, creating design documentation and style guides, and measuring post-launch performance.

  • Developer handoff
  • Design system documentation
  • QA & design review
  • Post-launch monitoring
