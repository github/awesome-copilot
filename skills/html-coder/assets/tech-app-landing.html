Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need equipment to use FitTrack?

No! We offer bodyweight workouts, as well as plans for home equipment or full gyms. Tell us what you have, and we'll customize your workouts accordingly.

Can I use FitTrack if I'm a complete beginner?

Absolutely! Our AI adapts to any fitness level. We'll start you with beginner-friendly workouts and gradually progress as you get stronger.

How does the AI Personal Trainer work?

Our AI analyzes your goals, fitness level, progress, and feedback to create personalized workout plans. It uses your phone's camera for form correction and adjusts difficulty in real-time.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! Cancel anytime through your app store subscription settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Does FitTrack work offline?

Yes! Download workouts and exercise videos to use offline. Nutrition tracking and basic features work without internet. Your data syncs when you're back online.

What devices are supported?

FitTrack works on iOS 14+ (iPhone and iPad) and Android 8+. We also integrate with Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, and other popular fitness wearables.
