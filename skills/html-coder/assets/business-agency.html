About Our Agency

Pixel Perfect is a creative digital agency founded in 2015 by designers and developers who believed that beautiful design and technical excellence could coexist.

Today, our team of 30+ creatives, strategists, and developers works with brands around the world to create digital experiences that drive results.

We've won awards, but we're most proud of the lasting relationships we've built with our clients and the impact our work has on their businesses.
