About Jordan

Hi, I'm Jordan! I've been a professional photographer for over 10 years, and I still get excited every time I pick up my camera.

My journey into photography started with a borrowed film camera and a college photography course. What began as a hobby quickly became a passion, and eventually, my career. I love that photography lets me meet incredible people, witness their most important moments, and create images they'll treasure forever.

My approach is documentary-style with an editorial eye—I want to capture genuine emotions and authentic moments, but with beautiful composition and lighting. Whether I'm shooting a wedding, a portrait session, or a commercial project, my goal is always the same: tell a story that resonates.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring New York's neighborhoods, trying new coffee shops, or planning my next travel photography adventure.

What I Believe

  • Authenticity over perfection: Real moments are more beautiful than posed ones
  • Connection matters: The best photos come from genuine rapport
  • Light is everything: Understanding light transforms good into great
  • Stories > shots: Every photo should tell part of a larger narrative
